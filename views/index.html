Hey there.

Hi! I'm Patrick(or PWee!), an undergraduate Computer Science and Economics student at New York University's honors college in Abu Dhabi.

I've been fortunate to travel to over 25 countries and experience many cultures, perspectives, people, work, challenges, and adventures. I have lived in China, The Philippines and the UAE. I'm currently studying in NYU Florence.

My academic and research background is in the relationship between technology and economic development. My professional background is in business management, public relations and web development.

I've interned at IBM and Fujitsu, as well as Hill+Knowlton and WRG. I've also served as a social media associate for a national senatorial election campaign. I've also co-organized the National Olympiad for Informatics in the Philippines.

I love to go scuba diving, play golf, and run. I'm also fluent in English, Mandarin Chinese, Tagalog (Filipino) and Taiwanese (Hokkien). I'm blessed with good friends and love to discover people's life stories.

I'm really passionate about Technology, Innovation and Entrepreneurship. This is my portfolio. If there is any way you think we can work together on something, I'd love to know. Shoot me an email at patrick.wee@nyu.edu or use the form below. :)
